# Sprint

Athleties Games

This is also known as Dashes. It is all about running and finish the sprint to a certain distance within the stipulated time. In professional sprinting, sprinters take a crouching position at the starting block and start forwarding with a gunshot and reach the finishing line. These are of 100 meters, 400 meters etc. in the international events. It is generally an Indoor game.

# Relay race

Athleties Games

The relay race is a type of race where a team has to be made and everyone has to complete their portion of the race with an object in the hand and then has to pass it to the other person. This game is all about the coordination between the team members. Everyone has to be calm in their mind to complete their own portion of responsibility.
